Kifo Ethiopian

Kitfo is a traditional meat-based dish of the Ethiopian culture, and one of the most popular dishes in Ethiopia. Renowned for its unique and flavorful taste, Kitfo is a spicy minced beef dish made with Niter Kibbeh (Ethiopian clarified butter), garlic, chili powder, and cardamom. This flavorful combination creates a unique dining experience that has been enjoyed for centuries in Ethiopia
Kitfo can be served well-cooked, lightly cooked, or even raw depending on your preference. It is often accompanied by a side of Injera (a type of flatbread made from teff flour), and Ayib ( Ethiopian cottage cheese). It is an important part of the cuisine in Ethiopia and can be found in many Ethiopian restaurants around the world.
